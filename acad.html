ACADEMICS

A Commitment to Excellence

COMPUTER SCIENCE:

IIITNR's Computer Science Department offers hands-on exposure with the most up-to-date programming languages and technology. We have IT experts who work directly with our students to ensure that they are ready for the real world.

ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATIONS:

A Communication Lab is located in our Electronics and Communication Engineering Department. This communication lab provides undergraduate and graduate students in electronics and communication engineering with the required hardware and software resources to help them improve their communication skills.

DATA SCIENCE AND ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Our College's Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Department has a Data Science Lab that allows students to not only practise basic programming in C, C++, and JAVA, but also to learn advanced computer science technologies such as Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Data Mining.
