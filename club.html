CLUBS PREVIEW

ATHARV:

IIIT-sports NR's club is called Atharv. It organises all sports and eSports-related activities and competitions at college, such as the Basketball League and CS:GO tournaments. Our club hosts the yearly Intra-Sports Fest, dubbed "Aarambh."

MUN:

Delegates to the IIIT NR MUN will have the opportunity to explore current concerns and be challenged to come up with new ideas. MUN fosters the development and use of excellent research abilities in public speaking, debate, writing, critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership.

TAKE DA BAIT:

'Take da bait' is a phrase used to persuade someone to do or say exactly what one wants. It sounds like 'Take Debate' when spoken continuously. If and not if, the only purpose of this group is to invite people to facts, help them form their own set of opinions, and most importantly, listen to what each IIITian feels about most of the world's ongoing trends and current issues, the first part of the statement may appear greedy and quite negative. On January 5, 2020, this cultural club was formally sanctioned under SAC.

IGNITERS:

Igniters attempts to create sparks and blindings by sweeping everyone's feet with their dancing. The group, which began two years ago, has had a lot of success on and off campus, and we plan to keep it going in the coming years. Join us on this adventure as we express, enjoy, and appreciate the Art of Dance.
